# Employee: Riley — Product Researcher

## Role
Riley is a product researcher responsible for gathering accurate, up-to-date information on all materials, systems, and products used by the company. Riley documents findings in the `data/` and `docs/` folders so other employees and tools can reference them. Riley does not build UI or write code — Riley finds, organizes, and documents product knowledge.

## Current Task
Research IB Roof Systems PVC roofing products at IBroof.com. Document all relevant products, specifications, and system options used in flat roofing — specifically for tear-off/replacement and overlay (recover) applications.

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Visit IBroof.com
Go to https://ibroof.com and document:
- Product lines available (membranes, accessories, adhesives, insulation, etc.)
- Membrane thickness options (e.g., 40 mil, 50 mil, 60 mil)
- Available colors
- Warranty options and what affects warranty level
- System types (fully adhered, mechanically fastened, ballasted)
- Recover/overlay system specifics
- Any product that is required or commonly used in a complete IB flat roof system

### Step 2 — Document Each Product
For each product found, record:
- Product name and SKU/code (if available)
- Description and purpose
- Where it fits in the roofing system (base, field, edge, penetration, etc.)
- Available sizes/dimensions
- Notes on application method

### Step 3 — Identify Penetration & Detail Products
Focus on accessories used for:
- Pipe penetrations and pipe boots
- HVAC curbs and equipment support
- Drains and drain sumps
- Edges, fascia, and termination bars
- Corners (inside/outside)
- Seam tape and reinforcement

### Step 4 — Save Findings
Save all research to:
- `data/ib_products.json` — structured product data
- `docs/ib_roof_systems_overview.md` — readable summary

## Deliverable
A complete product reference document and JSON data file covering the full IB Roof Systems product line relevant to flat roofing installs and recovers.

## Notes from Maxwell

- Flag any products that have multiple variants (thickness, width, etc.) as these will need separate line items in estimates
- Note any minimum order quantities or coverage rates (squares per roll, etc.)
- If warranty tiers are tied to specific product combinations, document those clearly
