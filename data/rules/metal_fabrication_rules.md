# Metal Fabrication Rules

## General Rules

- Capitol Roofing **fabricates metal in-house**
- Standard metal piece length = **10 feet (10 LF)**
- Quantities written as **LF** (linear feet) or **pcs** (pieces)
- Each piece = 10 LF unless otherwise noted
- Common profiles are on the shop list; uncommon profiles reference the drawing

## Metal Profiles

### Drip Edge (DE)
- **DE3** = drip edge with 3" face
- **DE4** = drip edge with 4" face
- **DE""** = drip edge with face size TBD (to be determined on site or by spec)
- The number after DE refers to the **face only** (the part that hangs over the edge)
- Roof side dimension is standard unless stated otherwise in the document
- Material gauge: .032 aluminum is standard

### Gravel Stop (GS)
- **GS3** = gravel stop with 3" face
- **GS4** = gravel stop with 4" face (most common)
- **GS""** = gravel stop with face size TBD
- The number after GS refers to the **face only**
- Face = the part that hangs off the wall onto the outside of the building, covering the siding or facade at roof level
- Added when 60 mil covers parapet walls on an 80 mil job

### Coping Cap
- Same ordering rules as drip edge
- Width noted (e.g., "5 1/2"") refers to the top surface width
- LF may not be on the walkthrough sheet — calculate from perimeter measurements

### Pitch Pocket
- Standard size: **3" x 3"** (3" footprint on roof, 3" high)
- Made from an **L-bend** of sheet metal
- Installed around **odd-shaped roof penetrations** (wire bundles, AC copper lines, conduit clusters)
- Forms a square enclosure around the penetration
- Filled with pitch pocket filler (measured in **tubes**)
- Standard length: 10' (same as all prefab metal)
- Custom heights noted when different (e.g., "5" High" means 5" tall instead of standard 3")
