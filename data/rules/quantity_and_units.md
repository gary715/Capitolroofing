# Quantity and Unit Rules

## Standard Units

| Unit | Meaning | Context |
|------|---------|---------|
| SQ   | 1 square = 100 SF | Roof area measurement |
| SF   | Square feet | Area calculations |
| LF   | Linear feet | Metal, edge, flashing |
| pcs  | Pieces | Individual items (each piece = 10 LF for metal) |
| rolls | Membrane rolls | See membrane_rules.md for coverage per roll |
| tubes | Tubes of sealant/filler | Pitch pocket filler, caulk |
| bags | Bags (adhesive, fastener bags) | Context-dependent |

## Metal Quantity Rules

- When LF is written: divide by 10 to get number of pieces
- When pcs is written: multiply by 10 for total LF
- **Every metal piece = 10 LF** (standard fabrication length)
- LF may be omitted on walkthrough when profile is uncommon — crew refers to drawing
- If LF is missing, it must be calculated from perimeter measurements or field dimensions

## Membrane Quantity Rules

- **50/60 mil:** 5.4 SQ per roll (540 SF), rolls are 6' x 90'
- **80 mil:** 3.6 SQ per roll (360 SF), rolls are 6' x 60'
- Account for **6" overlap** on every seam — reduces effective coverage
- Net coverage per roll (after lap deduction) should be calculated during estimating

## Filler and Sealant

- Pitch pocket filler: pourable urethane, measured in **bags** (1 bag fills ~6 standard pitch pockets, 4 bags per case)

## Items NOT Tracked

- **Unistrut** — steel channel for electrical/HVAC mounting. Rarely used, not part of standard roofing estimates. Omit from material lists.
