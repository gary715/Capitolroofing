# Membrane Rules

## Roll Specifications by Thickness

| Thickness | Roll Width | Roll Length | Coverage (SQ) | Coverage (SF) | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|---------------|---------------|-------|
| 50 mil    | 6'        | 90'         | 5.4           | 540           | Standard commercial |
| 60 mil    | 6'        | 90'         | 5.4           | 540           | Wall flashing / detail work |
| 80 mil    | 6'        | 60'         | 3.6           | 360           | Premium / high-traffic |

## Installation Rules

### Overlap and Seaming
- All rolls installed with **6" overlap** (lap)
- **2" barbed seam plates** installed under the 6" lap
- Seam is **heat welded** with a hot air gun to seal

### 80 mil Specific Rules
- **T-patches required** at every seam intersection
  - Example: 10 runs of 6' membrane + wall flashings = T-patch at every intersection point where horizontal and vertical welds meet
  - T-patches prevent the intersecting weld from opening
- 80 mil rolls are shorter (60' vs 90') — affects material quantity calculations

### Parapet Wall Rule (80 mil jobs)
When a job calls for 80 mil membrane AND has parapet walls:
1. Install **80 mil in the field** (flat roof area)
2. Install **60 mil on the walls** — easier to work with on vertical surfaces
3. 60 mil runs **6" onto the field** of the roof (overlap onto the 80 mil)
4. Add **Gravel Stop (GS)** to the top edge of the wall

**Example:** 30' x 60' building with parapet walls on all sides
- Field: 80 mil membrane
- Walls: 60 mil membrane, lapping 6" onto field
- Edge: GS at top of wall

## Square Footage — Field vs Material

- **Insulation SQ** = 2D field measurement only (flat roof area, no walls)
- **Material SQ** = field + walls (perimeter x wall height added to field area)
- Work order SQ is always >= sketch SQ because work order includes wall coverage
- This is why 326 Bloomfield shows 10.09 SQ on sketch but 18 SQ on work order
